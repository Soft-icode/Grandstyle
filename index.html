Get To Know Us

Grandstyle Entertainment Company is a record label and Media company, whose primary aim is to develop, promote and distribute media content. The company also provides multimedia (audio,video and photo) services to cooperate organisations, individuals and groups. We have world class facilities to produce audio materials such as Music, voice-over, audio books, radio commercials or jingles, radio programs and anything that has to do with audio. Our video services use only high definition cameras, experienced cameramen, video directors and video editors. We have very good photographers who are dedicated to bringing out beautiful images. Our graphic designers are very competent to bring our clients imagination to reality. We are time and productivity conscious, reasonable time delivery and conscious of clients satisfaction.
